How to delete video files from VPlayer?

Now you can delete files from iTunes libs, click on the icon on the top left of the screen, open the drop-down menu, select iTunes Libs, then click the icon on the top right of the screen, a menu will appear on the bottom of the screen with two options, select all and delete, select the checkbox next to the video you want to delete, then hit on the delete option on the bottom menu.
